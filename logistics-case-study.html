The Problem: Fragmentation in Delivery Tracking

Manual scheduling and tracking in courier services often lead to a 45% higher error rate in delivery updates. As the Product Lead, I was tasked with digitizing this lifecycle to provide real-time visibility for dispatch managers and customers.

Product Execution

I led the end-to- charcoal end development from user story mapping to launch, maintaining 100% on-time feature delivery across all Agile sprints.

The PM Approach

  • 1 User Story Mapping: Translated complex dispatch workflows into actionable user stories and acceptance criteria.
  • 2 End-to-End Delivery: Coordinated with engineering and design to manage the full product development lifecycle.
  • 3 QA Testing & Validation: Led manual QA testing sessions to ensure the platform met all functional requirements before the pilot launch.

Technical Documentation

By producing high-quality PRDs and TRDs, I ensured clear communication between technical and non-technical stakeholders, minimizing development roadblocks.
