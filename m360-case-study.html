M360 Suite: Consolidating Fragmented Marketing Operations

Role

Lead Product Manager

Impact

30% OpEx Reduction

Scope

Strategy to Launch

Users

500+ Internal/Client

The Challenge: High Operational Overhead

The marketing team was struggling with "Toggle Tax" constantly switching between five different tools for SEO, Email, Social, and Ads. This fragmentation led to data silos, duplicated manual work, and a $2,500/month overhead in redundant software subscriptions.

The Goal: Build a unified "Source of Truth" that automates workflow and consolidates billing into a single, scalable SaaS ecosystem.

The Core Solution

A modular platform architecture that integrated SEO tracking, automated email triggers, and AI-driven social scheduling into one dashboard.

The Approach: Data-Driven Prioritization

With so many features requested (Ads, SEO, Social, Content Gen), the risk of "scope creep" was high. I utilized the RICE framework (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort) to sequence the roadmap.

  • 01. Discovery: Shadowed 10+ team members to identify that "Reporting" was the biggest time-sink (taking 6 hours/week).
  • 02. Technical Discovery: Worked with Lead Engineers to evaluate API constraints for third-party integrations (Google Ads, SendGrid).
  • 03. MVP Phase: Focused on the Unified Dashboard and SEO module first to prove immediate value.

Navigating Technical Complexity

A major challenge was the sync frequency of the SEO and Ads data. Real-time updates were too expensive for the server budget.
