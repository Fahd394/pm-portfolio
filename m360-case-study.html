M360 Suite – Multi-Module Marketing Platform

Full case study showing end-to-end product ownership, problem solving, and impact.

← Back to Portfolio

Problem / Context

Marketing teams struggled with inefficiencies, duplicated work, and limited visibility when managing campaigns across multiple platforms. M360 Suite was built to provide a unified, scalable solution, combining ads management, SEO, community management, email automation, social scheduling, and automated content generation in a single platform.

Role / Scope

Owned product strategy and execution end-to-end, including problem definition, roadmap prioritization, stakeholder alignment, and delivery. Acted as the primary bridge between business, design, and engineering teams.

Approach / Solution

  1. Problem Framing: Defined clear problem statements aligned with business goals and user pain points.
  2. Discovery & Validation: Conducted user interviews, workflow analysis, and competitive research.
  3. Strategic Prioritization: Applied impact vs. effort and business value frameworks to sequence delivery.
  4. Cross-Functional Execution: Worked closely with design and engineering to translate strategy into execution-ready requirements.
  5. Iterative Delivery: Shipped in phases, incorporating feedback and performance insights.
  6. Measurement & Optimization: Defined success metrics and used early signals to refine the roadmap.

Outcome / Impact

  • Delivered a scalable platform supporting multiple user segments and workflows.
  • Improved operational efficiency and reduced manual processes across teams.
  • Established a foundation for future feature expansion and monetization.
  • Created clear visibility into performance and decision-making for stakeholders.

Lessons Learned

- Early user involvement and feedback is critical to avoid feature bloat.
- Clear prioritization frameworks help balance business goals with user needs.
- Structured communication with cross-functional teams ensures smoother delivery and faster iteration.
